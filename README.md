# Towards Robust Newton’s Method Optimization using Evolutionary Strategies
Sudhan Chitgopkar (`sudhanchitgopkar[at]g.harvard.edu`), Michael Zhan (`michaelzhan[at]g.harvard.edu`)
AM 205, Advanced Scientific Computing. Harvard University, School of Engineering & Applied Science.

We seek to combine the high-level approach of evolutionary strategies (ES) with the quadratic convergence and local robustness of Newton’s method to develop a robust hybrid approach. Here, we develop and test this approach against other common optimization methods on a series of benchmarks. We discover that these hybrid methods are less sensitive than Newton’s method to initial conditions yet reach higher accuracies than ES alone, at the slight expense of more iterations and run time. Though optimization algorithms are objective function-dependent, we are confident that the general trend holds for our hybrid method.
